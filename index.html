What Are Animations?

Animations are visual changes that make elements move or change appearance over time. They bring websites to life by adding movement and interaction.

Timing Functions

Timing functions — often called easing — control how an animation changes speed as it runs. Without them, motion moves at one constant rate, like a robot sliding from point A to B. With easing, animations feel smoother and more natural. They can ease in slowly, coast to a stop, or bounce a little for fun. CSS includes built-in options like ease, linear, and ease-in-out, or you can create custom motion using cubic-bezier() for full control over the curve.
